Welcome!

Welcome to the theoretical Mechanics, Engineering and Robotic Systems science department of the National airspace University named after Nikolai Zhukovsky "KhAI"!

This information-consult portal is intended for the students studying disciplines "Theoretical mechanics", "Theory of machines and mechanisms", "Machine elements and design principles" etc.

We try to be abreast with time and to give our students not only the best developments of the modern science, but also the up-to-date methods of their and our work organization. Starting this portal we hope that due to him will draw together teachers and students and they’ll be always not only in a contact but also on connection.

From the side we will endeavour maximally to satiate space of portal with methodical literature (both having "paper" analogues and not published in the usual understanding), to take part in the educational problems discussions, in good time to react on asked questions.

In addition, the >curriculum curriculum of employments and consultations is here resulted on a department, and also system of on-line record on consultation to those teachers which work on this system.
